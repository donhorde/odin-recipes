Lutefisk

Description

Lutefisk is cod that was was traditionally dried for storage through the winter. Then when it was time to use it, it was soaked in a mixture of lye and water which rehydrated and softened it.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound dried lutefisk
  • 1 quart water
  • 1 Â½ tablespoons salt
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted

Steps

  1. Bring water and salt to a rolling boil. Add lutefisk and return to a boil. Remove from the heat, cover, and let sit for 8 minutes. Check with an instant-read thermometer to make sure the internal temperature has reached 140 degrees F (60 degrees C).
  2. Carefully remove lutefisk from water using a scoop strainer or skimmer spoon. Cover with melted butter and serve immediately.
